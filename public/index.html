IFC Affiliate and Strategic Partner We are an investee of the World Bank Group’s International Finance Corporation with a 11-year proven track record investing in distressed asset portfolios and special situation investments in the region.
Altus Capital is a specialist investor and manager, focused on special situation and structured finance investments in South East Asia
The firm, set up in 2005, started out advising on and managing distressed credit portfolios in the Philippines and Thailand on behalf of institutional investors looking for exposure in these markets.
Special Situations & Structured Finance
Altus Capital’s Special Situations platform focuses on three core strategies across a wide variety of industries, geographies, and capital structure positions from senior loans to equity.
Real Estate
Altus Capital’s Real Estate strategy focuses on brownfield developments and re-development opportunities for commercial real estate in prime locations.
Digital Private Equity
ADC is the digital division of the ALTUS Group. Many interesting companies in Emerging Markets lack the capability to build and execute a digital strategy for their core business, yet this has become an essential ingredient for long-term success.
The Team
The Altus Capital team is comprised of highly skilled professionals with core competencies in and around the credit and real estate fields, with experiences that span the globe. We have brought on expertise in the digital and technology space with the launch of our digital private equity practice.
